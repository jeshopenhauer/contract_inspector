Article 4: Restrictions

3

FA SEP 2021


===PAGE_BREAK===
The provisions of this Agreement shall not be construed as implying an obligation of either
Party:

a) to provide any Confidential Information to the other Party, whether at its own
initiative or at the request of the other Party;

b) to enter into any further contract with the other Party in relation with the Purpose or
otherwise;

c) to propose or to purchase products to/from third parties.
