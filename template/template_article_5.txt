
Article 5: Ownership of the Confidential Information/Results


5.1        Any and all rights, title and interest in the Confidential Information of a Party shall remain its exclusive property. Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed as conferring or granting to the Receiving Party any intellectual or industrial property right, license, or right to use whatsoever the Confidential Information, either express or implied, other than for the use provided hereunder in relation to the Purpose.


5.2        The results generated in relation to the Purpose shall be the sole property of Valeo who has the sole right to use them and/or to proceed, at its sole discretion, with the registration of any patent or other intellectual property rights relating thereto.
